Traveling

Since the start of the Covid-19 pandemic, not as many people have been able to escape the UK or where they live to go and explore the world. There are so many fantastic locations to go and explore and if Covid has taught us anything, it is that we must enjoy our freedoms. Therefore it is the idea behind this quiz, to ask a few simple questions to provide new inspirational ideas and places for people to go and explore.

The Quiz

Just a note

First off, this is just a little quiz for fun, to try and let you explore new places in the world you may not have considered exploring previously.

How to Begin

It's simple, in the top bar, just click where it says start Quiz and your off and running.

What happens next?

Once you start the quiz, there is no time limit or anything, each page will ask you a question and you will have a few different answers to pick from, each of the answers has a title, a short description and then a picture, below this there is a box for you to click if you wish to chose that option as your answer.

What If I make a mistake?

Thats fine, we all get to change our minds, simply click in the top menu area, where it says "Go Back A Question" this will take you back one single question, if you want to go back further then you will need to click it again, until you get back to the questions you wish to go back to.

What if I want to share the results with someone else?

You can absolutely do this, simply copy the web address in the top bar our your browser and send it to who ever you like via any platform (Social media etc) you like, the results can be shared with whoever you want.
